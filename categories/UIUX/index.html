About



Chi Su is a 3D / UX Design professional with extensive training in 3D animation. She graduated from School of Visual Arts in New York in 2017. Currently she is making a transition to UI/UX field. She is a quick learner, highly motivated, detail oriented, problem solver, and loves to take on challenges. In her spare time, she focuses on refining her skills as a UI/UX Designer, keep up to date with the latest trends for Graphic and Interactive Design, and learning to code better.
